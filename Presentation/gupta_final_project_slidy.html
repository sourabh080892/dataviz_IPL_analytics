How Batting affect the winning Probability

  • Staying on the wicket and keep scoring runs is very crucial for any team

    • If individual score of any player in the team crosses 50; the probability of winning the match becomes more than 65%
    • If there is a successful partnership between 2 players exceeds 60 runs; the probability of winning the match becomes approx 90%

How First 5 and Last 5 overs score affect the win

  • Making good score in early stage of the match increases chances of winning

    • First 5 overs are very useful for any batting team and team scores more than 50 runs, which is not easy, probability of winning becomes more than 80%
    • Last 5 overs; every team tries to score as much they can and some times they loose wickets as well due to this
    • If any team scores more than 60 score in last 5 overs probability of winning becomes more than 65%

How Bowling affect the winning Probability

  • Taking wickets is very important for any bowling team

    • In the early stage of match, if bowling team gets success in taking only 1 wicket, probability of winning becomes 60%
    • If Bowling team gets more than 2 wickets in first 5 overs of match, they have even high probability of winning
